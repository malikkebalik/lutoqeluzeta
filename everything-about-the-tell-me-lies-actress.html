Everything About The Tell Me Lies Actress

September 03, 2024
blog

Natalee Linez is an American actress whose passion for acting led her away from Orange County to Los Angeles. After working jobs at a restaurant to pay for her acting classes, she finally got a job in the TV Series Siren.

Having a modeling and commercial background helped seal her stint in the movie industry. Linez has gone on to be featured in more series and movies over the years. She has also started her Wellness and Mental Health projects and enjoys some quality time with the love of her life.

Learn these Enthralling Details About Natalee Linez

1. She worked as a Hostess

Natalee Linez was born on October 25, 2000, in Orange County, California, United States of America. Many sources cite that she is a graduate of both San Juan Hills High School and Saddleback College.

She was born to Charly Baboff Linnes and is 1 of 3 siblings.

With a big dream, Natalee Linez (Linnes) moved to Los Angeles from California before she turned 20. She began pursuing her dreams of acting from then. However, way back, she had posed as a model for a couple of brands in the United States especially some in her native town.

While chasing her acting dreams, Natalee had to take up a job as a restaurant hostess in Beverly Hills. She needed the funds to be able to pay for the acting classes she enrolled in.

2. She had Her first Role on the TV Series, Siren

Eventually, Natalee’s dreams began to pay off when she landed her first role on the hit series created by Freeform titled Siren. On the series, she had 12 episodes playing the role of Nicole Martinez.

From there, she began to get noticed by other directors and producers who began to cast her in their movies and series.

She was cast as Nuyorican Music Manager in the 3rd part of Power Book as well as Lydia Montgomery in Tell Me Lies.

3. She is Psyched about Wellness and Mental Health and Launched a Company in Regard

Natalee Linez isn’t only about movies and modeling. She has proven that she can be more by launching Magic Mornings. Magic Mornings is a mental health and wellness company that Natalee uses to help people improve their well-being.

As a wellness and mental health enthusiast, Linez has been sharing helpful and tested routines with people. She also encourages the practicing of positive habits early in the morning and through the course of the day.

4. Linez is an Ambassador at School on Wheels

In tune with her knack for offering her services and help to all who need it, Linez took up a volunteer job at ‘School on Wheels’.

She serves as an ambassador for the non-profit cause aimed at carrying out projects for the homeless in Southern California.

According to the Siren actress, she also tutors a homeless child once a week and shares some love and positive vibes with the child.

Her aim for being part of this is to inspire the younger generation to never give up on their dreams. She believes that these kids can achieve whatever they set their minds to and she shows this belief by spending time with the kids.

5. Natalee Linez Has a Podcast

One more business venture up the 23-year-old’s sleeve is her podcast. Linez started the Ground Up podcast in January 2023. This podcast is one of the many channels Natalee uses to share her knowledge on mental health.

She invites and features guests on the podcast where stories and experiences are exchanged in a bid to inspire others to live well.

6. The Actress is in a Relationship

Natalee Linez hasn’t been quite open about her relationship status over the years. However, she has been more open to the idea of flaunting her man, in recent times.

On March 9, 2023, Linez shared videos of a man on her Instagram page. Over the next couple of months, she has continued to share their TikTok videos.

The man has been identified as Brandeaux Tourville, brother to Patrick Tourville.

Aside from being in front of the camera for Natalee’s TikTok videos, he has also been seen helping behind and in front of the scenes for the actress’ podcast and other projects.

The exact date Natalee started dating Brandeaux is unknown but it could probably be 2023 or a bit earlier.

7. Natalee Linez has Been in a Commercial for Microsoft

American actress and model has been featured in a couple of commercials in the past. On her Instagram profile, she shared videos of her in commercials for Microsoft, AT&T, and Farmers Insurance.

Her commercial stints came during her days of working as a model and just before getting her big break on TV.

Natalee still models occasionally as she combines all her passions and activities to form the super-American actress she is.
