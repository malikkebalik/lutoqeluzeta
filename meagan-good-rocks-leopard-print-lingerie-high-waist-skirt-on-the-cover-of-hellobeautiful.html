Hollywood's Meagan Good looked every bit captivating in the new update she posted, showing off how she aced her graceful appearance on Hello Beautiful's magazine cover.

Star actress Meagan Good's catchy quote on the cover of Hello Beautiful's new issue was as compelling as her stunning image that graced the cover page. Good looked every part the Hollywood Amazon in a tasteful ensemble.

The star channeled an animal-print glam, including her strappy dress that featured a bra top and high-waisted skirt. Good flaunted her ample cleavage.

Meagan Good at the Official Luda Day Party in Atlanta, Georgia, in 2019. | Photo: Getty Images

The star rocked black leather elbow-length gloves while her skirt bore a plunging opening that showed her flat tummy. Good tied her hair with an animal print scarf leaving the knot at the front.

The "Think Like A Man" actress completed her high-end look with a neck collar while posing for the camera with the right amount of elegance. The bold pink letters on the cover showed Good's empowering words. She captioned:

"Humbled to be on the cover of @HelloBeautfil March "Power" issue…"

During the interview, Good was as candid as she can get opening up about her early Hollywood career and the early realization that the showbiz industry required hard work.

Her words on the page read, "To be a powerful Black woman is to be unapologetic," and true to this, Good, 39, showed resilience in the industry, and now she is a seasoned star with expert knowledge in acting, producing, and designing.

Good decried the stigma that came with not knowing if motherhood was for her.

Despite putting up a solid front showing she couldn't be fazed, the award-winning actress weathered some tough moments. This, she shared, was mostly borne out of criticisms.

The star relayed that she could see scores of uplifting comments, but a few negative ones always got to her so much that she would question herself and always feel the need for validation. However, she learned to wave it all off.

These days, the 39-year-old is thriving and has been married to the love of her life, DeVon Franklin, since 2012. She and Franklin are also presently gearing to expand their family.

Three years ago, Good froze her eggs because she did not have the adoration of becoming a mom. However, it seems she is ready to switch and explore motherhood after all.

Once speaking about her earlier decision, Good decried the stigma that came with not knowing if motherhood was for her. She noted that this came off weird to many people.

The star noted that from her experience, motherhood was not always in the picture because she was a tomboy. However, it seems times have changed, and she is ready for that path.
