Tiffany Thornton is an American actress, radio personality, voiceover artist, writer, and singer who is known for playing Tawni Hart in the Disney Channel sitcom Sonny with a Chance as well as in its spinoff So Random!. She has also been cast in numerous other projects such as Kick Buttowski: Suburban Daredevil, Pixie Hollow Games, Desperate Housewives, American Dreams, Hannah Montana, That’s So Raven, The O.C., Wizards of Waverly Place, The Dog Who Saved Easter, Fish Hooks, Doc McStuffins, Game Change, Hatching Pete, 8 Simple Rules, Unlucky Charms, and Jericho.
