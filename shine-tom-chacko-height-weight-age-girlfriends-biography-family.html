Shine Tom Chacko is an Indian actor and former assistant director who has predominantly worked in Malayalam cinema. He started his career by working as an assistant to director Kamal and then after 9 years, he branched out as an actor. Over the years, Shine Tom Chacko has been a part of a number of films such as Nammal, Gaddama, Salt N’ Pepper, Ee Adutha Kaalathu, Chapters, Annayum Rasoolum, 5 Sundarikal, Hang Over, Arikil Oraal, Ottaal, Kaanchi, Konthayum Poonoolum, Pakida, Varnyathil Aashanka, Kammatipaadam, Masala Republic, Style, Ottakoru Kaamukan, Kurup, Viswasam Athalle Ellam, Ithihasa, Saigal Paadukayaanu, Kettyolaanu Ente Malakha, Ann Maria Kalippilaanu, Dum, Godha, Dooram, Koppayile Kodumkaatu, Popcorn, Prethamundu Sookshikuka, Mannankattayum Kariyilayum, Avarude Raavukal, Parava, Maniyarayile Ashokan, Oru Kaatil Oru Paaykappal, Who, Love, Kayamkulam Kochunni, My Santa, Bhoomiyile Manohara Swakaryam, Anugraheethan Antony, Wolf, Operation Java, Bheeshma Parvam, Beast, Bhramam, and Bheeshma Parvam.

    Shine Tom Chacko Facts

  • In 2002, he made his first film appearance as a bus passenger in the Malayalam-language romantic comedy-drama film Nammal.
  • Shine Tom Chacko co-starred as a petty thief named Alvy Benedict in the 2014 Malayalam-language fantasy comedy film Ithihasa, inspired by the 2002 American comedy film The Hot Chick.
  • Along with 4 women, he was arrested in Kochi on January 31, 2015, for allegedly possessing narcotics and a case was registered against him under the Narcotic Drugs and Psychotropic Substance Act. The High Court of Kerala later granted him bail.
  • He brought to life the character of Alwin in the 2019 Malayalam language romantic thriller film Ishq. For this performance, Shine Tom Chacko won the SIIMA Award in the “Best Actor in a Negative Role (Malayalam)” category.
  • Alongside Mammootty, Easwari Rao, Ranjith, Arjun Ashokan, and Jacob Gregory, Shine Tom Chacko co-starred as HDR Jojo Samson in the 2019 Malayalam-language black comedy film Unda. This role got him nominated for a CPC Cine Award for “Best Actor in a Character Role”.
  • He was cast as Bhasi Pillai/Pillaichan in the 2021 Malayalam-language crime thriller film Kurup, starring Dulquer Salmaan.
  • In 2022, he made his Tamil-language film debut by playing the role of a terrorist in the action comedy film Beast. The film stars Vijay and Pooja Hegde.
  • Shine Tom Chacko co-starred as Peter Anjootti (older son of Mathai and Molly) in the 2022 Malayalam-language action thriller film Bheeshma Parvam.
