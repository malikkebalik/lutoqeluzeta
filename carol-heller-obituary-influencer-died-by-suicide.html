Carol Heller Obituary, Death – On October 16, 2017, Carol Ann Heller, age 76, who was a devoted wife, mother, and grandmother in the Fayetteville area, passed away. She was born on May 10, 1941 in Big Springs, Nebraska, to the late Floyd Pearl and Margaret Alice Soper. Her parents have now passed away.
