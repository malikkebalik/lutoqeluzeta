Yeat Makes Rare Mask-Less Appearance At Portland Trail Blazers Game

July 06, 2024

Yeat stepped out without his signature mask this week to sit courtside at a Portland Trail Blazers NBA game.

The popular rapper was spotted with his girlfriend Symone as his home team took on the Phoenix Suns on Tuesday (December 19) – seemingly proving to be a good luck charm as the former took home the W.

The NBA team repped the talent on their TikTok page, posting a video with the caption: “YEAT in the house [fingers crossed emoji]”

Yeat was recently profiled for the Winter 2023/2024 issue of 032 magazine, opening up in a feature appropriately called “American Truths.” In one quote, the music maker shared that he’s not the biggest fan of words.

“I think I can communicate better with numbers than letters sometimes,” he shared. “I mean, on certain days there are no words for me at all. […] For example, I don’t send addresses, I just send coordinates. I can just look at the numbers and know where it is, based on the equator.”

He went on to explain that his preferred method of information exchange even extends to his life as a budding entrepreneur with multiple businesses.

“Often the numbers also relate to some of my businesses and companies,” Yeat said. “I actually have a few, but people don’t know about them because they’re privately held, and I don’t want to deal with shareholders.”

In another outtake shared by Kurrco, Drake‘s new protégé also reaffirmed his previous claims that he was abducted by aliens as a kid.

“That was deadass. It was real,” Yeat said. “I won’t go into too much detail because I’m not sure if they want me to talk about this right now. The aliens, I mean. I will say one thing though – they are really tall – and looked almost human.”

He continued: “I remember everything on the night that it transpired, second for second. I can tell the difference between a dream and reality, and I know I was awake. That’s also my first memory. Everything before that feels fake. Sometimes I think I might not even be from here, because I have dreams about other planets.”
