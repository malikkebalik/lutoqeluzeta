Zach Roerig Had Sights For Stardom Since Early Age

Born on 22 February 1985 in Montpelier, Ohio to Andrea and Dan Roerig, Zach Roerig attended Montpelier High School in his hometown and later Barbizon Modeling and Acting School in Cleveland.

Zach participated in International Model and Talent Association, through which he signed with his talent manager, which eventually paved his way to the stardom.

Soap Operas To Ladies' Heartthrob, How Is Zach Roerig's Career Shaped!

Roerig started his career with the legendary CBS daytime drama As the World Turns in the role of Casey Hughes. He played the role for a couple of years during which he garnered considerable praise from the show's audience.

After departure, he joined another long-running soap, One Life to Live, in which he played Hunter Atwood. His career's height, however, came with the role of Matt Donovan in The Vampire Diaries.

Only 33 years of age, Zach has earned the massive net worth of $4 million.
