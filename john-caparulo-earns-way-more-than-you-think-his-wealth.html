John Caparulo Earns Way More Than You Think; His Wealth!

Known as Mr. Cap by the comedy worshippers, John Caparulo is an American Standup comedian, notable in his E! talk show titled Chelsea Lately.

Throughout his 20-year-long journey, Caparulo has established himself as one of the finest comedians in the country. Consequently, he earns a tremendous value of net worth for all of his efforts. But, how did John Caparulo get at such a high level of stardom and fortune? Why not re-live his career?

Million Times Two

The Cap earns a staggering net worth of $2 millions as of 2019. Unlike many other comedians, the Mad Cap worked constantly in the field despite a lot of critics and did not give up in his articles.

So, how did the boy from East Liverpool made a lavishing life as a comedian?

Spreading Laughter Around the World

Caparulo collected most of his identity and wealth through his primary occupation as a comedian. In particular, he traveled around different locations in the world as a part of tours and shows.

YouTube: John Caparulo performing on Just For Laughs, Montreal International Comedy festival 2006.

Cap began his professional level of comedy at Just for Laughs festival in 2003 at Montreal, appearing in a sitcom show and The Late Late Show with Craig Kilborn.

The 42-year-old is a regular star at Harrah’s in Las Vegas each Thursday, Friday, and Saturday. Moreover, the humorist has all his weeks booked at Harrah’s and Goodnights Comedy club for the rest of this year.

On-screen Humor

Ever since he stepped into this career, John appeared in different television shows occasionally with the most notable ones being ESPN25: Who’s #1? , Comedy Central‘s Premium Blend, and Comedy Central Performs: John Caparulo.

The stand-up artist owned the privilege to perform alongside Hollywood stars like Jennifer Aniston, Pamela Anderson in The Tonight Show with Jay Leno from 2005 to 2008. Furthermore, the comedian’s a regular at the E!’s hit show Chelsea Lately from 2008 to date.

Tales n Sales

Caparulo also sells different CAP merchandises on his official website that sell from $2 to $12. Comic books and his Dvd’s hold majority of his sales. In addition to that, magnets, caps, t-shirts, and boxers, surprisingly are up for sale too.

Girlfriend turned Wife

Caparulo successfully turned his long-term relationship with Jamie Marie Kelly to a legit marital agreement at Water Street Tavern in Kent, Ohio on May 27, 2012.

The 44-year–old’s life long profession was successful to create yet another memorable moment. As he proposed his lover to marriage during his show at Comedy & Magic Club in California.

Happy Little Family

The beautiful happy duo shared a milestone as they welcomed their daughter Madden Jae on May 22, 2015. The Cap regularly features her cute little angel on his social media handle and his YouTube podcast as she grows up.
