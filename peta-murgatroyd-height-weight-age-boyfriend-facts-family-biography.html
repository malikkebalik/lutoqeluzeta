Peta Murgatroyd is a New Zealand-born Australian professional Latin dancer best known for her appearances on the American edition of Dancing with the Stars, which she has won twice with her celebrity partners Nyle DiMarco and Donald Driver. In addition to that, she has also performed in the international tour of the dance production Burn the Floor, including its Broadway run, as well as has been part of shows like The Ellen DeGeneres Show, Why Women Kill, Access Hollywood, Good Afternoon America, The Insider, Extra with Billy Bush, and Home & Family.

    Peta Murgatroyd Facts

  • She was born in New Zealand but relocated to Australia when she was just 18 months old.
