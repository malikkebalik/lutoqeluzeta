Alahna Ly Was A Passionate R&amp;B Singer Since A Young Age

August 28, 2024
blog

An uprising singer, Alahna Ly, had a passion for singing from a young age. No surprise that she was only in her teens when she released her first R&B song. But, how old was she exactly, and what is her age now?

Get acquainted with her details, including age, height, and also songs!

Alahna Ly Started Making Songs From Her Teenage Years

Ly had her singing talents from the very first; she received the first prize in a 9th-grade talent show after singing. Thankfully, her skill was accompanied by her passion for music.

In 2016, she released her first original song titled “The Gold” through Soundcloud. At the time, she was only at the age of 16. The same year, she collaborated with JUAN PALACIOS for the song “Kiss n Tell” and with Campaign Vier for the song “Hot.”

After that, Ly began uploading cover versions of songs like Stay, Superficial Love, and Marvin's Room on her Soundcloud account. Only in 2017, she released her next original Summertime via Soundcloud.

A year later, at the age of 18, she collaborated with The Lane Pavers to produce her first music video, ‘Bailando.’ The song was released through YouTube on August 7, 2018---It already has more than a million views now.

Alahna Ly Created Self-titled YouTube Channel At 19

Ly created an official self-titled YouTube Channel on February 24, 2019, apparently to pitch her musical career.

She released her original song, Helluva Night, through the channel on February 6, 2020. After nine months, on November 25, 2020, she released another song titled Why.

Within a year—with only three videos to date—her channel has more than four thousand subscribers and more than 100 thousand views. No wonder she can make it big in the field with consistent dedication.

How Old is Alahna Ly?

As of 2020, Ly is only 20 years old. The American singer was born on March 11, 2000, in Royal Oak, Michigan.

Her zodiac sign is Pisces. 

Alahna Ly's Height Is Surprisingly Small!

Alahna looks to be of an average height in the pictures she posts on her social media handles.

However, a picture posted on her Instagram on December 13, 2017, surprisingly revealed that she is not as tall as her photographs depict. The photo featured Alahna standing beside a statue of the world’s tallest man and a height-measuring stand. And, her height was just a little more than four and a half feet.
