Aurora Teagarden Mysteries Order, Watch Movie Chronologically, by Release Date

  • How to Watch Aurora Teagarden Mysteries in Order

    With more than dozen movies and countless murders solved, Aurora Teagarden Mysteries is one of Hallmark’s most popular franchises. But if you’re starting from the beginning (or have missed a film or two in between), you may be wondering how to watch Aurora Teagarden Mysteries in order to see the evolution of Lawrenceton’s beloved amateur true crime investigator. 

    Aurora Teagarden Mysteries franchise premiered its first movie, Aurora Teagarden Mystery: A Bone to Pick, on Hallmark Movies & Mysteries in 2015. The series is based on Charlaine Harris’ 11 mystery books of the same name. The books, which were published between 1990 and 2017, follow Aurora Teagarden, a professional librarian at Lawrenceton Public Library who runs the Real Murders Club, a group of true crime fans who come together to study famous crimes throughout the history of their small town.

    The series starred Full House alum Candace Cameron Bure in 19 movies from 2015 to 2022. Bure was recast by Lifetime movie actress Skyler Samuels, who plays a younger version of Aurora Teagarden, in 2023 after Bure left Hallmark for Great American Family. 

    “The opportunity of getting to kind of reinvent and reimagine this beloved character in her earlier years was really fun, and a new creative challenge I haven’t gotten to do before. It was an exciting job to take on and it’s been a joy,” Samuels told Media Village in June 2023 of taking over the role of Aurora Teagarden. 

    So what are the Aurora Teagarden Mysteries movies in order? Read on for how to watch the Aurora Teagarden Mysteries franchise by release date. 

    The Aurora Teagarden Mysteries movies are available to stream on Hallmark Movies Now. 

  • Aurora Teagarden Mysteries: Something New (2023)

    Aurora Teagarden Mysteries: Something New is the 19th movie in Hallmark’s Aurora. Teagarden Mysteries franchise and the first without Candace Cameron Bure. The film — which premiered on June 9, 2023 and stars Skyler Samuels as a younger version Aurora Teagarden — sees Aurora in her 20s as she investigates the murder of a person suspected to be killed by her best friend Sally’s fiance. The film, which is the first prequel to Bure’s Aurora Teagarden Mysteries movies, sees Aurora investigate the murder in hopes of clearing Sally’s groom as the main suspect.

  • Aurora Teagarden Mysteries: Haunted by Murder (2022)

    Aurora Teagarden Mysteries: Haunted by Murder is the 22nd movie in Hallmark’s Aurora Teagarden Mysteries franchise and the last film starring Candace Cameron Bure. The movie — which premiered on February 20, 2022 — follows Aurora Teagarden as she investigates a murder in a house considerred haunted by Lawrenceton locals. The house is also where Aurora and her best friend, Sally, found a body as teenagers.

  • Aurora Teagarden Mysteries: Honeymoon Honeymurder (2021)

    Aurora Teagarden Mysteries: Honeymoon, Honeymurder is the 17th movie in Hallmark’s Aurora Teagarden Mystery franchise. The film — which premiered on August 22, 2021 — sees Aurora and her husband, Nick Miller, go on a “pre-honeymoon getaway.” But while they’re on vacation, Aurora and Nick discover a body. As they investigate what happens, danger literally knocks on their door. 

  • Aurora Teagarden Mysteries: Til Death Do Us Part (2021)

    Aurora Teagarden Mysteries: Til Death Do Us Part is the 16th movie in Hallmark’s Aurora Teagarden Mystery franchise. The film — which premiered on November 17, 2021 — starts with Aurora Teagarden getting ready to marry the love of her life, Nick Miller. But when a body is discovered days before her wedding, Aurora fears that her father, charles, may be a suspsect, as she and Nick race to solve the case before they walk down the aisle.

  • Aurora Teagarden Mysteries: How to Con a Con (2021)

    Aurora Teagarden Mysteries: How to Con a Con is the 15th movie in Hallmark’s Aurora Teagarden Mysteries franchise. The film — which premired on March 14, 2021 — sees Aurora Teagarden investigate the death of one of her mother Aida’s real estate clients with help of her fiance, Nick Miller. 

  • Aurora Teagarden Mysteries: Reunited and It Feels So Deadly (2020)

    Aurora Teagarden Mysteries: Reunited and It Feels So Deadly is the 14th movie in Hallmark’s Aurora Teagarden Mysteries franchise. The film — which premiered on October 18, 2020 — follows Aurora Teagarden as she investigates a murder at her 20th high school reunion. There are plenty of suspects but no witnesses as Aurora must find out who’s lying and who isn’t. 

  • Aurora Teagarden Mysteries: Heist and Seek (2020)

    Aurora Teagarden Mysteries: Heist and Seek is the 13th movie in Hallmark’s Aurora Teagarden Mysteries franchise. The film — which premiered on May 17, 2020 — follows Aurora Teagarden as she and the Real Murders Club investigate the theft of a priceless crown stolen at a benefit gala held by the Lawrenceton library Roe works at. 

  • Aurora Teagarden Mysteries: A Very Foul Play (2019)

    Aurora Teagarden Mysteries: A Very Foul Play is the 12th movie in Hallmark’s Aurora Teagarden Mysteries franchise. The film — which premiered on August 18, 2019 — follows Aurora Teagarden and the Real Murders Club as they stay in a resort for a true crime convention. While there, they take part in a murder mystrey dinner, but when art imitates life and fellow actor is killed, Aurora and the gang must investigate the death.

  • Aurora Teagarden Mystery: An Inheritance to Die For (2019)

    Aurora Teagarden Mystery: An Inheritance to Die For is the 11th movie in Hallmark’s Aurora Teagarden Mystery franchise. The film — which premiered on August 11, 2019 — starts with Aurora sevving as a bridesmaid for the wedding of her close friend Lizzie. But Lizzie’s aunt dies from poisoning at the nuptials, Aurora and the Real Murders Club must investigate how one couple’s happy day could turn into one of their worsts. 

  • Aurora Teagarden Mysteries: A Game of Cat and Mouse (2019)

    Aurora Teagarden Mysteries: A Game of Cat and Mouse is the 10th movie in Hallmark’s Aurora Teagarden Mysteries franchise. The film — which premiered on August 4, 2019 — follows Aurora Teagarden as a taunting clue invites her to a cat-and-mouse game with a mysterious criminal who commits increasingly serious crimes that leave Lawrenceton in fear.

  • Aurora Teagarden Mysteries: The Disappearing Game (2018)

    Aurora Teagarden Mysteries: The Disappearing Game is the ninth movie in Hallmark’s Aurora Teagarden Mysteries franchise. The film — which premiered on July 29, 2018 — sees Aurora investigate the disappearnces of her nephew and his roommate following the suspicious death of the roommate’s girlfriend. To help her, Aurora enlists the Real Murder Club, as well as her handsome new neighbor, college professor Nick Miller.

  • Real What You Sew: An Aurora Teagarden Mystery (2018)

    Reap What You Sew: An Aurora Teagarden Mystery is the eighth movie in Hallmark’s Aurora Teagarden Mystery franchise. The film — which premiered on April 15, 2018 — sees Aurora Teagarden investigate the death of her old friend and internet sensation, Poppy, who returned to Lawrenceton to start an embroidery business. As Aurora and the Real Murders Club investigate the crime further, they learn that not everyone was happy with Poppy’s success, as they end up with four differents suspects with four different motives. 

  • Last Scene Alive: An Aurora Teagarden Mystery (2018)

    Last Scene Alive: An Aurora Teagarden Mystery is the seventh movie in Hallmark’s Aurora Teagarden Mystery franchise. The film — which premiered on January 7, 2018 — starts with a crew arriving in Lawrenceton to film a new Hollywood movie. But when the lead actress of the film is found poisoned and bludgeoned in her dressing room, Aurora Teagarden and the Real Murders Club must investigate what really happened.

  • A Bundle of Trouble: An Aurora Teagarden Mystery (2017)

    A Bundle of Trouble: An Aurora Teagarden Mystery is the sixth movie in Hallmark’s Aurora Teagarden Mysteries franchise. The film — which premiered on May 21, 2017 — sees Aurora host her close firned Martin’s niece and her newborn for the night in her home. But when mystery strikes — Martin’s niece disappears, the newborn is found hidden in the bathtub and Aurora discovers a stranger in he linen closet with $10,000 in cash and a diaper bag — she’s called to her investigative talents to find out what went wrong. 

  • Dead Over Heels: An Aurora Teagarden Mystery (2017)

    Dead Over Heels: An Aurora Teagarden Mystery is the fith movie in the Aurora Teagarden Mystery franchise. The film — which premiered on March 19, 2017 — sees Aurora investigate the murder of Detective Sergeant Jack Burns, a local police chief she never got along with but didn’t expect to find dead. 

  • The Julius House: An Aurora Teagarden Mystery (2016)

    The Julius House: An Aurora Teagarden Mystery is the fourth movie in Hallmark’s Aurora Teagarden Mystery franchise. The film — which premiered on October 16, 2016 — starts with Aurora Teagarden buying a house where the previous tenants, the Julius family, disappeared four four years ago. Not being able to forget the family who lived there before her, Aurora and her friends in the Real Murders Club start investigating their disappearance and what really happened to the Julius family.

  • Three Bedrooms, One Corpse: An Aurora Teagarden Mystery (2016)

    Three Bedrooms, One Corpse: An Aurora Teagarden Mystery is the third movie in Hallmark’s Aurora Teagarden Mystery franchise. The film — which premiered on June 12, 2016 — starts with Aurora Teagarden offering to help her real estate agent mother, Aida, show a house. But when a handsome client finds the body of a rival realtor in the backyard, Aurora must investigate what happened as suspicion falls on everyone in the real estate community. 

  • Real Murders: An Aurora Teagarden Mystery (2015)

    Real Murders: An Aurora Teagarden Mystery is the second movie in Hallmark’s Aurora Teagarden Mystery franchise. The film — which premiered on July 26, 2015 — sees Aurora host a murder mystery club named the Real Murders Club at the library she works at. But when she discovers that people connected to the club are being killed off one by one in copycat murders of the true-crime books the group discusses, Aurora takes it upon herself to investigate who the real murderer is. 

  • Aurora Teagarden Mystery: A Bone to Pick

    Aurora Teagarden Mystery: A Bone to Pick is the first movie in Hallmark’s Aurora Teagarden Mystery franchise. The film — which premiered on April 4, 2015 — sees local librarian Aurora Teagarden, also known as Roe, as she inherits the house of a member of her true crime club, the Real Murders Club. But when Aurora discovers a skull inside the home, she learns that there’s a real mystery to solve.
