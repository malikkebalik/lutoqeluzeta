SZA And Justin Bieber’s Early Friendship

SZA and Justin Bieber first met in 2014, when they were both featured on the song “Love Galore” by Travis Scott. They quickly became friends, and Bieber has been a vocal supporter of SZA’s music ever since. In 2017, he even called her “one of the greatest artists of our generation.”

Rumors of a Romantic Relationship

The first rumors of a romantic relationship between SZA and Bieber emerged in 2018. They were spotted together at several events, and there were even reports that they were dating in secret. However, both SZA and Bieber denied the rumors, and they insisted that they were just friends.

Justin Bieber And Hailey Bieber’s Relationship Timeline

The relationship between Justin and Hailey Bieber has had its share of highs and lows since the beginning. It was in 2009 when they first made eye contact backstage at the TODAY Show. In spite of the fact that they initially denied having a romantic relationship, they spent the holidays together, and in 2015, Bieber acknowledged their relationship with an Instagram post. They persevered through health concerns, public displays of devotion, and rumors of engagement during their relationship.

In 2018, they took the next step and got engaged, which was followed by being married in a courthouse. In 2019, the pair wed in a ceremony that was more traditional in nature. They have managed to keep their relationship strong by standing by each other through good times and bad, and by commemorating the love they share over the course of their lives.
