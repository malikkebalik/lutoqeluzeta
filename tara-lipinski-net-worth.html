Tara Lipinski Wiki Biography

Tara Kristen Lipinski was born on 10 June 1982, in Philadelphia, Pennsylvania USA. Tara is a famous former figure skater best known as the youngest Olympic Gold medalist in skating in 1998, and also for winning the World Champion in 1997. Despite the success she had, Lipinski decided to retire from her competitive career in 1998.
