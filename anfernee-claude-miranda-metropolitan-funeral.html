Anfernee Claude Miranda Metropolitan Funeral

August 06, 2024
blog

Anfernee “Ant” Claude Miranda was born June 20, 1996 in Dangriga, Belize to Loretta Antonio Miranda and Claude Miranda.

Ant was educated at James Hurst and Victory Elementary Schools, Cradock Middle School, and I. C. Norcom High School.

He worked in the government sector as a U. S. Postal Service worker for over 5 years.

Ant  loved working with those he’d grown to call friends. He enjoyed spending time with his family and friends. He loved reading and listening to music.

Ant touched many lives; he knew he was loved and you knew he loved you. He was preceded in death by  his grandfathers; Mr. Elliott Antonio, Mr. Cesar Miranda, his uncle Lenoard Miranda. Ant leaves behind to cherish his memory, his brother Stephon Miranda, sisters Loryece and Aada Miranda, nephew Noah Joseph Miranda. Grandmothers, Mrs. Monica Antonio and Mrs. Matilda Miranda. Extended family, Wasani Miranda, Jada and Patrick Garcia and other family and friends.

Ant will truly be missed by all.
