Usher Has 2 Sons with Ex-Wife Tameka Foster and They Look Just like Their R&amp;B Singer Dad

July 16, 2024
blog

The "Confession" coroner, Usher Raymond, has two young boys with his former wife and celebrity stylist, Tameka Foster, and they are nothing but his spitting image.

Usher has had to appear in a courthouse several times over the custody of his sons after his split from his ex-wife, Tameka, but that did not contest with the love he possesses for his kids.

It is, in fact, the fatherly love that fuelled his drive to want a life with his children, Usher V and Naviyd Ely.

Usher attends the Little Kids Rock Benefit 2019 at PlayStation Theater on October 10, 2019 | Photo: Getty Images

The Raymond boys are not only their father's pride, but they share his looks too. In celebrating his second son when he turned 10, the R & B singer updated his Instagram with a photoshoot of himself and the young blood.

The father-son twinned in similar outfits, and their looks were not only thing to notice from this gesture, as the father of two expressively poured out his wishes and thoughts for the birthday boy.

Usher did not try to mince words when describing Navyid's flaws and perfections. He went on to describe the 10-year-old as a reminder of his younger self on character traits. He wrote,

"One thing everybody looks for, everybody can appreciate, and everybody needs from time to time. You are that for me , and then some."

Navyid's brother also got as much love from their father. On his 11th birthday, the singer charged Usher V to take up the responsibility of being himself, and he told the world just how much he appreciated the introduction to fatherhood via the birth of his oldest child.

In 2009, during advocacy for Boys & Girls Clubs of America, the father of two shared how his older son takes after his personality and how he wishes Usher V finds passion in whatever career choice he chooses in the future.

Usher and Tameka went their separate ways in 2009 after a two-year marriage, but have maintained cordiality for their boys. Last year, Tameka on her Instagram page, made and posted a collage of Usher's images in celebration of his birthday.

The mother of two wrote a lengthy message in celebration of her former husband's birthday, and fans of the "U Gat It" singer, believed it was an "I miss you" sign.

Although the award-winning musician is married to his longtime beau, Grace Miguel, both Usher and the mother of his sons are doing fine parenting their boys.
