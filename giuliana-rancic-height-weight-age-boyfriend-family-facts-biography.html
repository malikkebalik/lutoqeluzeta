Giuliana Rancic is an Italian-American entertainment reporter and television personality who is best known for being a co-anchor of E! News. She has also appeared in a variety of other shows and films such as Fantastic Four: Rise of the Silver Surfer, Bring It On: Fight to the Finish, Giuliana and Bill, Are You Smarter Than a 5th Grader?, After Lately, Fashion Police, E! True Hollywood Story, Chelsea Lately, Ready for Love, The Stand In, and many more. She has published numerous books such as Think Like a Guy: How to Get a Guy by Thinking Like One and I Do, Now What?: Secrets, Stories, and Advice from a Madly-in-Love Couple. During the course of her career, Giuliana has been honored with numerous awards such as the Daytime Emmy Award.

  • Giuliana was born in Italy but relocated to the United States at 7 with her family.
