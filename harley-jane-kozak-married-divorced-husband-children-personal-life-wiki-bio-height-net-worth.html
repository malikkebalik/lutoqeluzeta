Who is Harley Jane Kozak?

Born January 28, 1957, Harley Jane Kozak’s birthplace is Wilkes-Barre, Pennsylvania, USA. Jane’s birth name is Susan Jane Kozak. She is the daughter of Dorothy,  a university music teacher, and Joseph Aloysius Kozak, an attorney. Her family moved to Lincoln, Nebraska after her father’s death. Kozak moved to New York after her high school and worked there as a waitress for 10 years.

She attended New York University‘s Graduate Acting Program at the Tisch School of the Arts and graduated from there in 1980. Kozak legally changed her birth name from Susan to Harley. She did so because she liked the name, and she also confessed that the name came from one of her boyfriends who owned a Harley-Davidson bike.

As of now, Kozak lives in Los Angeles, California.
